About Us

Welcome to High School

Located on a sprawling 3-acre campus in Kharghar, Navi Mumbai, A. C. Patil College of Engineering (ACPCE) prides itself on its ultra-large infrastructure, providing an ideal environment for both academic and extracurricular activities. Established in 1992 by the esteemed leader, Honourable Shri Rohidasji Patil, under the aegis of Jawahar Education Society, ACPCE has risen to become a premier technical institute in the region.

Our best Cources

Civil Engineering

Explore the realm of civil engineering where you will learn to design, construct, and maintain essential infrastructure such as buildings, bridges, dams, and transportation systems. This field emphasizes sustainability, safety, and efficiency in urban and rural development projects, preparing you to address the infrastructure needs of communities globally.

Computer Engineering

Merge the principles of electrical engineering and computer science in computer engineering, specializing in hardware-software integration. You will learn to design and develop computing systems, from microprocessors to network infrastructure, driving advancements in artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies.

Read More

AI & Data Science

Immerse yourself in AI and data science engineering, where you will develop expertise in designing algorithms, analyzing large datasets, and building intelligent systems. From machine learning to predictive analytics, this field is essential for driving innovation in healthcare, finance, autonomous systems, and beyond, shaping the future of technology-driven industries.

Read More

Electrical Engineering

Enter the realm of electrical engineering, focusing on the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. This discipline covers a wide range of fields, including power generation, transmission, renewable energy systems, telecommunications, and electronic circuit design, preparing you for a career at the forefront of technological innovation.

Read More

Information technology

Master the fundamentals of IT engineering, encompassing the development, implementation, and management of computer systems, networks, and software applications. As technology evolves, IT engineers play a crucial role in optimizing business operations, cybersecurity, and digital transformation strategies across various sectors.

Mechanical Engineering

Delve into the diverse field of mechanical engineering, focusing on the design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. From robotics to renewable energy solutions, this discipline equips you with the skills to innovate across industries, ensuring machines and systems operate effectively and sustainably.
